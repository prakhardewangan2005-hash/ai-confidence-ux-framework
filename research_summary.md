# Research Summary – AI Confidence Calibration Framework

## Overview
The study explores how AI systems can signal confidence to users effectively. Users often misinterpret AI certainty, leading to overtrust or undertrust, impacting decision-making and workflow efficiency.

## Methodology
1. Literature review on trust in automation and AI  
2. Behavioral analysis of user trust responses  
3. Risk perception mapping for AI predictions  
4. Framework design based on human-AI interaction principles

## Key Insights
- Users ignore textual confidence unless visually emphasized  
- Color-coded and icon-based indicators improve trust calibration  
- Enterprise users value reliability and contextual explanations over aesthetics

## Framework Application
The framework can be applied in:
- Enterprise AI dashboards  
- Customer support AI assistants  
- Predictive analytics systems  

## Future Enhancements
- Integrate feedback loops for adaptive confidence signaling  
- Conduct longitudinal studies for trust calibration impact  
- Extend to multimodal AI outputs (text, speech, visual)
